Q1 How can you list all files in a directory, with the most recent one displayed last?

ls -tr


Q2

How can you display the first 25 lines of a file?

head -25 file.txt

How can you display just lines 16 through 25?

head -25 file.txt | tail -10

Q3 How can you search for all commands relating to python? Hint - it will be a use of
“man”.

man python
